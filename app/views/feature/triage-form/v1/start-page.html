Get help finding apprenticeships with Early Connect

You can use this service to access apprenticeship matching services, to connect with relevant opportunities in your local area.

Use this service to:

  • ask for advise on choosing the right career
  • get support to find the right apprenticeship
  • link with employers in your area
  • ask for support with employability skills
  • ask for support applying and interviewing for an apprenticeship
{#

To apply you must:

  • be currently in Year 13
  • attend a school or further education collage within one of the three pilot regions; Lancashire, the North East and Greater London
#}

This service is free.

! Warning This pilot service is currently only available in three regions; Lancashire, the North East and Greater London, supporting students that are currently in Year 13.
